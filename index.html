VoxRecall AI Support

Private voice memory, searchable transcripts, and on-device audio workflows.

How to get help

Email support: support@voxrecall.ai

If email routing is not configured yet, contact the developer through App Store Connect review notes.

Common steps

  • Tap Start Recording to capture a voice memory.
  • Approve Microphone and Speech Recognition permissions when iOS asks.
  • Use Search to find spoken words after transcription finishes.
  • Open a memory to replay audio, copy transcript text, export, rename, or delete.
  • Change transcription language in Settings. Auto is the default.
