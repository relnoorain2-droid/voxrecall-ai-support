VoxRecall AI Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 30, 2026

VoxRecall AI is designed as a private on-device audio memory app. The app does not collect, sell, track, upload, or process your recordings on a server.

Data stored on your device

Audio files, transcripts, timestamped transcript segments, keyword indexes, settings, and exports are stored locally inside the app sandbox on your device.

No account, analytics, ads, or tracking

The app does not include an account system, advertising SDK, analytics SDK, third-party tracking SDK, cloud transcription service, or external AI service.

Permissions

Microphone permission is used to record audio. Speech Recognition permission is used to create searchable transcripts on device where supported by iOS.

Sharing and exports

Files leave the app only when you choose to share or export them using iOS sharing controls.

Deleting recordings

Deleting a recording removes the local audio file, transcript data, search index data, and related Spotlight index item.
